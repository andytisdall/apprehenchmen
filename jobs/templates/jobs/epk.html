Bio

Apprehenchmen is a four-piece rock band in Oakland, CA. Andy Tisdall, veteran of Bay Area bands Little Teeth and ZOO, started playing music with his fiance because they didn't want to hire a band at their own wedding. After their marriage, the couple began to write strange little indie rock songs to entertain friends. They recruited other Oakland musicians to fill out the band, and are now releasing their second full-length album. "Murder Eyes" is a 14 song examination of how just deep you can dive when the world has lost its mind.

Andy Tisdall - bass, vocals
Stacey Swan - drums, vocals
Dan Hoffheins - guitar
Shaun Haber - guitar
