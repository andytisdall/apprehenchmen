Bio

Apprehenchmen is a post-punk 4 piece band in Oakland California. Their new album "Norm-Core: Your Perfected Self" channels My Bloody Valentine, Gang of Four, Pavement and Brian Eno, often switching rhythms and genres within the same song to keep their listeners from getting comfortable. Anchored by the spousal team of Andy Tisdall and Stacey Swan, Apprehenchmen's eclectic live performances, striking recorded output, and inscrutable lyrics have earned them a devoted following in the East Bay.

Andy Tisdall - bass, vocals
Stacey Swan - drums, vocals
Dan Hoffheins - guitar
Shaun Haber - guitar
